The Development Process

STAGE 1

Requirements

This is the stage where you outline what your website needs. Our team will work with you to make sure these requirements are complete and clear before proceeding to the next step.

STAGE 2

Design

After gathering your requirements, we'll create a plan for developing your website. This usually consists of an image of what each page of the website will look like.

STAGE 3

Development

Once we've completed the website design, we'll move on to the development stage. Here, we'll write the necessary code for your website and set up any requested services.

STAGE 4

Testing

In this stage of the development process, we will test your website to ensure that it fits the requirements and there were no errors made while writing the code.

STAGE 5

Maintenance

This is the final stage of development, this is where we will provide small updates each month to ensure your website continues to run smoothly.
Please note, this stage being included for free is dependent on the pricing option you select for your website or a private agreement. Maintenance starts from £50 per month but can vary depending on the complexity of your website. We also do not provide maintenance for websites which we haven't developed or have since been modified by a third party. Free maintenance is not included for websites developed for free.

What We'll Need From You

Step 1: Confirming Your Identity

To use our services, you must be over 18 so you can agree to our terms and conditions. We also check your identity to protect us from abuse and fraud. So, in order to confirm your identity with our identity checking partner Checks Direct, we'll need a UK Government issued ID which is valid, not lost or stolen and not expired.

We will also need payment of our £10 identity checking fee. We accept:

If you are an Individual / Sole Trader:

  • UK Driving License
  • UK Passport

If you are a Company:

  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • The person who is authorised to act on behalf of your company must also have their ID verified with one of the accepted IDs above.

Step 2: Confirming Your Address

We also require that you confirm your billing address before using our services.

If you are an Individual / Sole Trader or a Company:

  • You may use a document which has your name on it (or your company's name) and your address (or your company's address). The document must have been issued within the last 3 months.

Examples Include:

  • Utility Bill
  • Bank Statement

Step 3: Requirements

We'll need some information about the requirements for your website. Once this stage has been completed, we will ask for payment to start the development process.
We typically ask for:

  • Website Colour Scheme (if applicable)
  • Any assets for example: Images, Icons, Logos etc.
  • Any designs you currently have for your website.
  • A description of your website's required functionality.
  • Your email address for communications

It is your responsibility to check that you have the intellectual property rights to use the assets you provide us. You must also give us full permission to use these assets within your website.

Step 4: Payment

Before we start developing your website, we must first accept payment for your chosen pricing option for developing your website. Please note there may also be separate monthly charges for certain online services and ongoing maintenance fees; our team will tell you this during the requirements stage. All fees will also be on your invoice. You will not be charged for online services or maintenance until your website is being used by its end-users.
You may need to raise your daily transaction limit on your bank account. We accept debit cards, credit cards, and bank transfers as payment.

Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How Long Will Each Stage of the Development Process Take?

This can vary based on the complexity of your website. However, generally it takes 1 week to write the requirements, 2 weeks for design, 2 weeks for development, and 3 weeks for testing. So, a total development time of 8 weeks (2 months). This also does not include non-working days like bank holidays and weekends.

Why do you offer free websites?

We offer free websites so you can get an idea of how the development process works and what our work looks like before committing to a paid plan. Therefore, if you like the work we have done, you can upgrade to a paid plan for additional features.

Do you accept customers outside of the UK?

Unfortunately, we do not accept customers outside of the UK. However, we are looking to expand into different countries in the future.
