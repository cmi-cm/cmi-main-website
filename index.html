How we measure success

We don't build for beauty. We build for results.

📊

Conversion metrics

Click-through rates, form submissions, cart abandonment, sales velocity. If it moves the needle on revenue, we track it.

💰

Revenue impact

Direct revenue increase, cost savings, margin improvement, lifetime value. Everything ties back to your bottom line.
